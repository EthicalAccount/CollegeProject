Panjab University opened a Regional Centre for imparting quality technical education to kandi area hoshiarpur with the name of Swami Sarvanand Giri Panjab University Regional Centre in year 2006 with four branches of engineering. In 2007 Panjab university started two new courses in LAW as BALLB 5 year integrated course and 3 Years LLB course, and Master of computer applications (MCA).

The vison of Regional Centre has been made keeping in mind Vision 2025 of Panjab University.

Vision:
To Be A Globally Renowned Education Hub that Caters Quality Education.

Mission:

  • To create a State-of-the-Art infrastructure that paves the way for new ideas to germinate and create Educationists, Researchers, Innovators, and Entrepreneurs who are compatible with the outside world at Ethical and Global Level.
  • To produce a talent that can understand, assimilate and solve the problems in the allied fields at the Local, National, and International levels.
  • To collaborate with Educational Institutes and Industries of National and International repute and provide necessary experiential exposure to budding talents.

PUSSGRC has Training, Placement & Industrial Liaison committee, headed by Mr.Rajeev Kumar Dang. As part of this committee, Dr. Balwant Raj is the overall Training Incharge. Committee has faculty members from each branch of Engineering and MCA. This committee looks after all Training & Placement related activities for the benefit of students. For efficient and smooth working of this committee, student committee is constituted which works under the guidance of faculty members. The student members from each department are selected on the basis of personal interviews conducted by faculty members. Besides creating different job avenues for the students, the committee ensures that students are well equipped to meet the requirements of the industry . For enhancing the employability of students, various workshops, seminars and expert talks are organized by this committee. Committee works in close liaison with Central Placement Cell, Panjab University Chandigarh. The students of the campus have been placed in reputed firms like Infosys, Tech Mahindra, Accenture, Quark, SML ISUZU, Mahindra & Mahindra, Ambuja Cement, Honda, Wipro, ITC, Infogain, L&T Infotech, HP. In addition to this, students of our campus are working in various Public sector undertakings, state services & armed forces. Every year students pursue higher studies in India and abroad.
